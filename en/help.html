Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Subscriptions in your account dashboard. Find your active subscription and click the "Cancel" button. The cancellation takes effect immediately, but you retain access until the end of the current billing period. No further charges will be made after cancellation. If you cannot find the cancel button, contact our support chat and we will assist you within 5 minutes.
Once your refund request is approved, funds are returned to your card within 5–10 business days, depending on your issuing bank. For digital wallets, the processing time is 1–3 business days. If more than 10 days have passed and you have not received your funds, please contact support with your transaction number. Refunds are only considered for transactions made within the last 30 days.
Yes, absolutely. All payments are protected by SSL/TLS 256-bit encryption and comply with PCI DSS Level 1 — the highest security standard for payment systems. We do not store your card details on our servers; all processing is handled through a certified payment provider. Your bank statement will show a neutral company name to protect your privacy.
Creator verification involves three steps: uploading a government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card), taking a selfie while holding the document, and completing your profile. Documents are verified through Onfido and permanently deleted from our servers after successful verification. The process takes 24–48 hours. You will receive an email notification with the result.
Content may be unavailable for several reasons: an expired subscription, geographic restrictions, or a temporary block due to scheduled maintenance. First, check your subscription status in your account dashboard. If your subscription is active, try using a VPN or changing your region in settings. If the problem persists, contact support with the content ID and a description of the error you are seeing.
Yes, one account supports simultaneous streaming on 2 devices under the basic subscription. When a third device connects, the oldest session is automatically ended. Premium subscribers can use up to 4 devices at the same time. All active sessions are listed under "Security" in your account settings, where you can remotely end any session at any time.
Tips are a direct way to support a creator. During a live stream or on a creator's profile, click the "Tip" button and select an amount (from $1 to $500). The amount is deducted from your wallet balance or card instantly. The creator receives 80% of the tip amount credited to their account. Tips are non-refundable once sent, so please confirm the amount carefully before submitting.
If you find content that violates our platform rules, click the "Report" button (flag icon) located below any piece of content. Select a reason from the list: illegal content, minors, copyright infringement, fraud, or other. Reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours. If a serious violation is confirmed, the content is removed immediately and the offending account is permanently banned.
